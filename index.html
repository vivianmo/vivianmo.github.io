About Me

Hello. My name is Vivian Mo and I am currently a sophomore at Princeton University majoring in Electrical Engineering and pursuing certificates in Applications of Computing and Robots and Intelligent Systems. I am interested in Android app development as well as incorporating software and hardware components into systems, such as in robotics or remote diagnosis. I am well-versed in Java, HTML/CSS, Javascript, and RobotC, have experience working with Bluetooth Low-Energy and Electric Imp, and am adept at using Android Studio, Adobe Photoshop, and Bootstrap.

As a designer, I appreciate the beauty in pixels. My interest in graphic design began in 2011 when I discovered and began making animated gifs. Since then, my gifs have accumulated over 2 million reblogs and likes on tumblr and have been featured on Buzzfeed, Reddit, Giphy, and Pinterest, just to name a few. Most notably, I invented the walkthrough gif, a giffing technique that has since been put into use by other prominent gifmakers.
