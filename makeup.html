Makeup and Flowers

Once again inspired by a National Geographic video, “Makeup and Flowers” began when I was watching a time lapse video of carnivorous plants and noticed that a Venus flytrap opening its jaws bore a remarkable resemblance to a mascaraed eye opening. Thus, I began juxtaposing and synchronizing the motion of plants with shots from high fashion makeup commercials into a mock beauty campaign series called “Makeup and Flowers”.
