Walkthrough Gifs

Invented by myself in 2014 under the tumblr url "crazychipmunk", the walkthrough gifs are a series of animated gifs in which two scenes of the same movie are synced up in way so as to give an illusions that characters literally "walkthrough" from one scenes to another. Usually, they feature a character or characters walking from an early scene in the movie to a later scene and growth seen between the different scenes is extremely apparent. For example, the most popular walkthrough gif involves Rapunzel picking up a book in her tower before falling in the arms of Eugene in the outside world, literally walking into fairytale. The walkthrough gifs were extremely popular, spanning numerous copycats and reposts.
